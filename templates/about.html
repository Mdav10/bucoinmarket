About BuCoinMarket

Our Mission

BuCoinMarket is a modern digital marketplace designed for businesses that want to move toward a cashless future. It combines an online store, a secure digital wallet, and an internal digital currency called BuCoin (BUC) into one platform.

What is BuCoin (BUC)?

BuCoin (BUC) is the official digital currency of the BuCoinMarket platform. It is not a cryptocurrency and it is not an investment. BuCoin is a digital payment currency that exists only inside the BuCoinMarket ecosystem.

How It Works

  1. Create a free account
  2. Receive your personal BuCoin Wallet
  3. Purchase BuCoins using supported payment methods
  4. Shop and pay with your BuCoin wallet

Benefits

  • Faster checkout
  • Reduced cash handling
  • Better payment records
  • Customer loyalty
  • Digital receipts
  • Real-time sales reports
